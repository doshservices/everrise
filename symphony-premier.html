About Symphony

Symphony The Movie is a first of its kind Nollywood musical starring heavyweight actors like Jackie Appiah, Kalu Ikeagu, Efa Iwara etc and Afrobeats maestro Dbanj.

Symphony follows the life of Slade, a young man in his early twenties who dreams of becoming a successful musician globally. Struggling to make ends meet, Slade strives to care for himself and his grandma.

Luckily he gets discovered on Social Media by a big-wig in the entertainment industry (Dj Bang) as he also charmingly secures the heart of the most important lady in his school, Lolita.

This movie tells the story about life, love, dreams, success, trials, societal failure and the resultant effects.
