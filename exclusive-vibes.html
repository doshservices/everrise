About Exclusive Vibes of the Year

EverRise Entertainment organized the Exclusive Vibes of the year party hosted by Dj Consequence.

The event which was sold out 5 days upon announcement was headlined by South Africa stalwarts - Dj Maphorisa and Virgo Deep

Including acts from Nigeria such as Tiwa Savage, Mayorkun, Peruzzi, Portable, Victory, Zlatan and a host of others.
