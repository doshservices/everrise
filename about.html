About Us

EverRise Entertainment

EverRise Entertainment is one of Nigeria’s top leading entertainment outfits. From organizing high profile, exclusive events to sold out concerts, the company has distinguished itself from others in the entertainment space in Nigerian and west Africa which is apparently the vision of the company.


EverRise Entertainment is also a trailblazer in the movie industry in Nigeria having successfully produced Symphony the movie- the first musical movie in the Nollywood industry.


The chairman of the EverRise group is positioning EverRise entertainment and it’s subsidiaries as a major industry player in the entertainment scene across the entire world.
